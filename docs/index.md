# Welcome to ZobTube

*Passion of the Zob, lube for the Tube!*

ZobTube is a self-hosted **video library viewer and management tool**. Organize your collection, browse videos, movies, and short clips, and enrich entries with actors, channels, and categories — all from a single binary that runs on your own hardware.

Starting with **0.6.0**, ZobTube can manage **photosets** — albums of still images alongside your video library. A photoset works like other library items: you can attach **actors**, **channels**, and **categories** at the album level, and override them on individual photos when needed.

Whether you are trying Docker for a quick trial or running the binary with SQLite on a home server, you stay in control of your data and your library layout.
